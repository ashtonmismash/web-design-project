Meet Chloe, the chat bot that will serve your everyday journaling needs.

She lives on your phone, in your messages so she is everywhere that you need need her to be.

to get started using the Chloe Journal chat bot get started with these simple steps!

 

Download Chloe

1

if you are not on your phone please load the website on your mobile device. Once you have the site loaded on your mobile device click the download button.

DOWNLOAD

Enable Chloe on your phone

2

Once you have Chloe downloaded on your phone open your messaging application. There should be a prompt from Chloe asking you for permissions, and acceptance of terms of service. Select yes to both.

Set your settings

3

Chloe will have a lot of settings that she is going to want you to specify. One expample of this is when you would like her to give you a notifcation to use the journaling app, or to turn off notifications entirely. These settings will make your experience with Chloe unique to you.

Go through the starter conversation with Chloe

4

Chloe is going to need to have a starting coversation with you to configure some settings for the application. Complete the starting questions that Chloe askes you, and that is the final configuration step for the platform.

Your all done!

5

You are all set with the Chloe Journal Chat Bot! If you are still having problems installing the chat bot on your phone check out our FAQ pages that will go indepth into more issues.
